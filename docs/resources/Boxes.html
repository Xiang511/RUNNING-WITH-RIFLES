Boxes

Boxes are very rare items that AI soldiers can drop on death. You sell a box to Armory to get a random reward. As a reward, you recieve two items: one from the Drop 1 list for the box, and the other from the Drop 2 list.

There are three types of Boxes: Gift Boxes, Community Boxes, and Seasonal Boxes. The first two always have a chance to drop, from any AI soldier. While Seasonal Boxes are only active during respective season (event), and may require killing a special enemy.

Percentages may not add up to 100% due to rounding.
